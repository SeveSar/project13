GroguToken Features

Project selection

Each Gaming and NFT project that is produced by our platform undergoes a thorough audit and selection. Our team checks the security of the project, the legitimacy of the team, the prospect of the project ideas.

Initial Game Offerings

We support blockchain games, provide financing, and also assist them in marketing. We provide our community to receive tokens of future promising blockchain games before they appear on the exchange.

Unified control panel

Access to games and game assets of any blockchain is carried out in a single user control panel. This is convenient both for players (getting access to games in one place, tracking the profitability of the game).
